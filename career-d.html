Production Worker (Factory)

Manufacturing, Food Industry  |  On Site  |  September 14, 2025
Apply Now
Full Time On Site
Job Description

We are looking for dedicated Production Workers to join our factory team in the food industry. You will be responsible for operating machines, assisting in the production line, and ensuring products meet quality standards. This role requires attention to detail, teamwork, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Key Responsibilities
  • • Operate and monitor machines on the production line.
  • • Assist in packaging and labeling products.
  • • Follow health, safety, and hygiene standards strictly.
  • • Inspect finished goods to ensure quality requirements are met.
  • • Report any issues with equipment or materials to supervisors.
  • • Work as part of a team to meet daily production targets.
Job Overview
  • Date Posted: September 14, 2025
  • Location: Factory - On Site
  • Experience: 0-2 Years
  • Gender: Both
  • Qualification: High School or Above
